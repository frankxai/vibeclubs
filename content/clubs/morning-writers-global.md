Writers-only. Your local 9am. Two 50/10 blocks. Café ambient on, voice
off. No chat, no critique, no workshop. Just words on a page.

Timezone-agnostic — there's a morning somewhere, always. The Meet
link rotates; the ritual doesn't. Show up with the doc open, hit start,
don't check Slack.

If you ship 800 words, that's a session. If you write two sentences
you keep, that's also a session. The cards count minutes, not vibes.
